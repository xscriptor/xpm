<h1 align="center">Code of Conduct</h1>

<p align="center">
  xpm is developed through open collaboration. We expect all participants to keep interactions respectful and inclusive.
</p>

<hr />

<h2 id="our-standards" align="center">Our Standards</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Be respectful, concise, and constructive in discussions.</li>
  <li>Focus on technical quality, security, and maintainability.</li>
  <li>Support contributors by sharing context and rationale.</li>
  <li>Encourage professional and collaborative problem-solving.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="unacceptable-behavior" align="center">Unacceptable Behavior</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.</li>
  <li>Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation.</li>
  <li>Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.</li>
  <li>Sharing private information without permission.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="reporting" align="center">Reporting</h2>

<p>
  Report incidents privately to <a href="mailto:x@xscriptor.com">x@xscriptor.com</a>.
</p>

<hr />

<p align="center"><b>By participating, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.</b></p>
