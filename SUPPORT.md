<h2 id="before-opening-an-issue" align="center">Before Opening an Issue</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Include your <code>xpm</code> version and platform details.</li>
  <li>Include the exact command executed and full output.</li>
  <li>If repository-related, include relevant <code>xpm.conf</code> repo block (without secrets).</li>
  <li>If dependency-related, include package names and expected resolution behavior.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="response-expectations" align="center">Response Expectations</h2>

<p>
  Issues with reproducible steps and clear environment context are triaged first.
</p>
